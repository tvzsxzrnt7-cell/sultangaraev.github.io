Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Time & Money handles information when you use the app. Time & Money is designed to keep your personal data on your device unless you choose to share, export, back up, or send it yourself.

Information stored in the app

Time & Money may store information you enter into the app, including shifts, tasks, habits, accounts, transactions, categories, planned payments, debts, subscriptions, notes, attachments, receipts, backup files, and app settings.

Local storage

Your app data is stored locally on your device. The developer does not operate a server that receives or stores your in-app financial, schedule, habit, task, or backup data.

Backups and exports

If you create, share, restore, or export a backup or report, that file may contain personal data from the app. You are responsible for choosing where to save or share those files. Only share backups and exports with people, services, or devices you trust.

Support emails

If you contact support by email, your email address, message content, and any information you choose to include may be used to respond to your request. Do not send sensitive financial data, backup files, or personal documents unless you understand the risks.

Purchases and subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your full payment card details.

Data sharing

Time & Money does not sell your personal data. The app does not share your in-app personal data with the developer’s server because no such server is used for storing app data.

Analytics and tracking

Time & Money does not use third-party advertising tracking. If analytics or crash reporting are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what is collected and why.

Children

Time & Money is not intended for children under 13. The app is designed for personal productivity and personal finance management.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page.

Contact
